The starting town of Ravencrest will be the first town that the player discovers. This is where the character will get introduced to the quest system and be able to take their first "Class Quest"

 

Discovering

The entrance of Ravencrest is randomly placed in a forest biome that is not the starting area. The entrance will have a Raven that can be interacted with the follow to discover Ravencrest.

 

 

 

NPCS

 

 Tharin - The Blacksmith (Melee)

Thrain is a burly dwarf with a long, braided beard and a bald head adorned with tattoos. He wears a sturdy leather apron over his muscular frame

Thrain sells the Item Level 1 Melee Weapons by Default

 

 

Elara - The Hunter (Ranged)

Elara is a lithe and agile elf with long, flowing auburn hair tied back in a practical braid. She wears a green tunic and leather pants

Thrain sells the Item Level 1 Ranged Weapons by Defaualt

 

 

Alaric - The Wizard (Magic)

Alaric is an elderly human with a long, white beard and a tall, pointed hat. He wears flowing, deep blue robes decorated with intricate silver patterns and symbols.

Thrain sells the Item Level 1 Magic Weapons by Default

 

 

Morgana  - The Witch (Summoner)

Morgana is a mysterious figure with raven-black hair cascading down her back and piercing green eyes. She wears a dark, flowing dress with silver accents and a wide-brimmed hat adorned with feathers.

Thrain sells the Item Level 1 Summoning Weapons (NOT Grimoire yet)

 

Garrick - King Slime Domain

After completing any of the Class Quests on your world you will now see Garrick appear in the Tavern of Ravencrest

Garrick will provide you the means to get to the King Slime Domain
