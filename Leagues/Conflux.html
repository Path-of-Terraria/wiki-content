Overview

In the Conflux League you will experience the fabric of reality tearing itself apart. Intruders from another realm begin invading your world looking for new ground to spread their dominion. These introducers invade through Rifts that can be found in your Explorable Maps. 

The overall intention of this league was to introduce more “Elemental” Aspects to the game. Furthering the idea of having elemental damage types

 

In Map Content

Rifts are “tears” in the fabric of the world. They appear 100% of the time while the “Conflux” league is active. 1–3 Rifts can spawn per map, scaling with map tier. There are 3 types of factions. One cold based, one fire based, one lightning based. They appear as unstable, glowing tears with unique effects (fire cracks, distortion, icy crystal fractures, etc. depending on the faction). Open them simply by clicking them. Upon opening them a new UI element for “Stability” comes up. This stability bar must be filled by killing enemies that are spawned from the rift. The higher the stability, the more “Conflux” is dropped. Conflux comes in 3 forms. Celestial, Infernal, and Glacial. The types of Conflux that drops is based on the rift that is opened. Rifts have a 2% chance to spawn in a mini-boss that has a 100% chance to drop some conflux for that type. They also provide a large boost to the amount of stability and conflux dropped in the end. Conflux is not a “actual” item. It is put into it’s own storage so that it does not take up any inventory slots. You can view your stored conflux at your map device. Conflux is “world based” storage. Meaning all players in a world share the conflux. Certain mobs will give more % to stability than others. Depending on the difficulty of these mobs. The rift closes after 30 seconds, OR at 100% stability. Whichever comes first. Reaching 100% stability should be very difficult at higher tier maps (9+) without significant build investment.

 

Aether Gates

Aether gates are opened by using the Elemental Conflux within the Map Device. The Aether gate opened is based on the conflux used to open the portal. Aether Gates are the space between the Invaders home reality and yours. These are what the invaders are using to travel to your reality from the Rifts. Upon fighting your way through the Aether Gates you find yourself facing one of the new unique bosses found in the Conflux League
