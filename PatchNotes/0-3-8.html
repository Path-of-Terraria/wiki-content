  • Rainbow Crystal Staff is 25% all elements & chaos

  • Spectre Staff is now 100% chaos damage (spoopy ghosts)

  • Adds in loot explosions to Boss Kills, with a heavier weighting towards currency
  • All area levels of bosses have been lowered some to accomodate for these new changes
  • Overworld now starts at Area Level 1
  • Tiers of maps now start at area level 71, and scale +1 per tier. Instead of starting at T12 after moon lord (to match our other post ML changes for mapping)
  • Wording of various things have been updated to be more consistent
    • + Level to
    • “multiplier” → “more"

 

Bug Fixes

  • Optic staff can now roll proper affixes
  • Raging Spirits can not only proc on MINION damage, not SUMMON damage. This will prevent recursive loops.
  • Fixes Critdamage displaying 0% in Stat-screen
  • Fixes Lethal Overdose Tooltip
  • Fixes bloodbender scaling via max bleed stacks
    • Bloodbender damage scaling changed from 10% to 2% to account for this.
    • Bloodbender internal cooldown is now 0.5 seconds.

 

 

Balance

  • Raging spirits damage nerfed to 50% from 100% of hit.
  • Bloodbender now has a short internal CD.
  • Shattered Bond reduced from 300% to 200% explosion damage
  • Monster experience is now always based on a monster's base health pool rather than hand-tuned per-enemy values. Tougher enemies naturally reward more XP.
  • HP buffs from map affixes or mob affixes (e.g. a monster rolling as Rare or Magic) no longer inflate XP beyond the rarity bonus — the XP comes from what the monster started with, not what it ended up with after scaling.
  • Rare monsters still grant +10% XP and Magic monsters +5% XP.
  • Map modifiers continue to boost XP as before.
