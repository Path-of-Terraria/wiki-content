Features

  • Abigails Flower is now 100% chaos damage (spooopy ghosts)
  • Bat Scepter is now 100% chaos damage (its a bat...close enough)
  • Hornet Staff is now 100% chaos damage (poison = chaos)
  • Queen Spider Staff and Spider Staff are now 100% chaos (spider = spooky chaos)
  • Rainbow Crystal Staff is 25% all elements & chaos
  • Shadowbeam staff is now 100% chaos damage
  • Drain Life is now 100% chaos damage
  • Spectre Staff is now 100% chaos damage (spoopy ghosts)
  • Vilethorn is now 100% chaos damage - Changes (hopefully all) summon weapons to be their own weapon type, as they were previously displaying as Grimoire. Fixed their affix pool.
  • Completely reworked how area level starts, and progresses. Overworld can now scale all the way to 70
  • Adds in loot explosions to Boss Kills, with a heavier weighting towards currency
  • All area levels of bosses have been lowered some to accomodate for these new changes
  • Overworld now starts at Area Level 1
  • Tiers of maps now start at area level 71, and scale +1 per tier. Instead of starting at T12 after moon lord (to match our other post ML changes for mapping)
  • Wording of various things have been updated to be more consistent
    • + Level to
    • “multiplier” → “more"
  • Adds in a basic loot filter system.
    • Can check item rarity (Normal, Magic, Rare, Unique)
    • Can check item level
    • Can check base type
    • Future improvements like affixes are planned

  • Raging Spirits can not only proc on MINION damage, not SUMMON damage. This will prevent recursive loops.

  • Fixes Lethal Overdose Tooltip
  • Fixes bloodbender scaling via max bleed stacks

    • Bloodbender internal cooldown is now 0.5 seconds.
  • Made Sniper to be actually multiplicative
  • Fixed Deep Pools reading all damage, but only increasing Magic Damage
  • Made Increased Damage while Stationary work with Melee
  • The following masteries were updated from multiplicative (unintended) to additive
    • Lethal Dosage
    • Increased Damage to Distant Enemies
    • Increased Damage while Stationary
    • Increased Minion Damage
    • Increased Sentry Damage
  • Fixes an exception that could happen during chest loot spawn causing the world to not load properly
  • Fat Bullet now scales additively
  • Fixed Fat Bullet causing Phantasm/Vortex Beater to rapidfire
  • Fixes the floating node bug and you are no longer able to deallocate a mastery
  • Fixes some other required allocated mastery issues, namely with the poison cluster and the gun cluster.
  • Fixes an issue with crafting items that would weigh similar results on the same exact item heavily

 

 

Balance

  • Raging spirits damage nerfed to 50% from 100% of hit.
  • Bloodbender now has a short internal CD.
  • Shattered Bond reduced from 300% to 200% explosion damage
  • Monster experience is now always based on a monster's base health pool rather than hand-tuned per-enemy values. Tougher enemies naturally reward more XP.
  • HP buffs from map affixes or mob affixes (e.g. a monster rolling as Rare or Magic) no longer inflate XP beyond the rarity bonus — the XP comes from what the monster started with, not what it ended up with after scaling.
  • Rare monsters still grant +10% XP and Magic monsters +5% XP.
  • Map modifiers continue to boost XP as before.
