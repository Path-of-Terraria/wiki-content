New

Added Energy Shield as a new defensive stat.

  • Energy Shield absorbs damage before health.
  • Energy Shield recharges after avoiding damage for a short time.
  • Added Energy Shield UI support, player stat display support, and hover text support.
  • Added visual effects for active Energy Shield and shield break moments.

Added new Energy Shield armor bases:

  • Apprentice Cap, Robe, Slippers
  • Acolyte Hood, Vestment, Sandals
  • Mystic Circlet, Garb, Boots
  • Spectral Veil, Shroud, Striders
  • Ethereal Diadem, Mantle, Greaves

  • Hallowforged Helm, Cuirass, Greaves
  • Astralforged Greathelm, Aegis, Sabatons
  • Eternalforged Greathelm, Aegis, Sabatons

Added new armor affixes:

  • Added maximum Energy Shield
  • Increased Energy Shield
  • Increased Energy Shield recharge rate
  • Faster start of Energy Shield recharge
  • Added maximum minions on body armor

 

Changed

Energy Shield armor now has Energy Shield as a base stat, similar to Defense.

  • Energy Shield no longer appears as an implicit affix on Energy Shield armor.
  • Existing Energy Shield armor values are preserved as the item’s base Energy Shield value.

Defense and Energy Shield item tooltips are clearer.

  • Modified Defense or Energy Shield values now turn blue.
  • Holding Shift shows the item’s base roll range when available.
  • Holding Shift also shows the original value and modifier calculation when affixes change the stat.

Energy Shield affixes now only roll on items that actually have Energy Shield.

  • This prevents Energy Shield affixes from appearing on unrelated armor or jewelry where they would not make sense.
  • Maximum minions can now roll as a body armor affix, but only on Path of Terraria body armor.

This prevents stacking vanilla armor set bonuses with extra minion affixes from the PoT armor system.

 

Visuals

  • Added item icons and equipment sprite sheets for the new armor bases.
  • Updated armor visuals so the new bases are easier to distinguish in inventories and while equipped.
