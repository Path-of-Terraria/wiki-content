Features

 

 

Bug Fixes

  • Updates all accessories that were classified as something other than an accessory back to an accessory.
    • This will prevent you from rolling shield mods on Shield of Cthulhu and Paladin Shield for example
  • Fixes long lived projectiles like yoyos not having dynamic damage
    • This means for things like Rage and Berserk - The damage of these projectiles are updated when buffs expire/enable to account for various outside buffs
  • Fix Pyralis not properly spawning projectiles, adjust their damage to be 300 consistently

Accessory Affix Rework

  • All accessories related to jump are now classified as such
  • Wings are now classified as such
  • Accessories no longer roll the normal affix pool
  • Accessories now roll only QoL (and some kinda power related) affixes
  • Wings have wing-related affixes to their pool
  • Jump Accessories have jump-related affixes added to their pool
  • Other accessories can roll all of these new affixes
