Features

  • Thunder Claps on the Nova Passive Tree now has a max level of 2
  • Added mechanics tags for intelligence, strength and dexterity
  • Disabled scrolling in the passive tree when holding shift to make mechanics tags easier to use
  • Localized & brightened the "(shift)" notice for inline mechanics tags
  • Added Reprisal (by GabeHasWon) and Increased Maximum Sentry Passive (by pilchardheck) sprites
  • Add in Spelunky mastery sprite (by GabeHasWon)
  • Adds the One Minion Army Keystone to the summoner tree.
    • "You are limited to one maximum minion, but gain 80% increased damage per maximum minion slot."
  • Adds the Direct Shot Keystone to the ranger tree.
    • "Your Projectiles deal up to +50% more Damage at Nearby range, but deal up to 50% less Damage at Far range"
  • Adds the Blood Magic keystone to the mage tree.
    • All of your mana costs become life costs. Gain 5% of the damage done as life back on all damage, scaling with your max hp. Short internal CD.
  • Adds the Cinder Bastion Keystone to the melee tree.
    • "Gain a 1% increase to Ignite damage per 10 maximum life. When moving, apply a 20% max life Ignite to yourself."
  • Ranged class now starts with the normal vanilla starter ammo

 

Bug Fixes

  • Updates all accessories that were classified as something other than an accessory back to an accessory.
    • This will prevent you from rolling shield mods on Shield of Cthulhu and Paladin Shield for example
  • Fixes long lived projectiles like yoyos not having dynamic damage
    • This means for things like Rage and Berserk - The damage of these projectiles are updated when buffs expire/enable to account for various outside buffs
  • Fix Pyralis not properly spawning projectiles, adjust their damage to be 300 consistently
  • Fixes increased skill damage passive providing double damage instead of intended percent damage
  • Reduced cooldown passive not using correct passives.json value and incorrect tooltip
  • Fixes Shock chance on nova not being calculated correctly
  • Fixes explorable maps and boss domain maps calculations for distribution not working as intended
  • Fixes an issue that would cause item rarity in chests found in map to always be normal
  • Three masteries (Center of the Universe, Automagic Missile, and Reverberation) were triggering on any damage type. They are now restricted to Magic only.
  • The ShockChancePassive was fixed to actually provide 10% as the tooltip suggested
  • Loot explosions no longer count segment boss NPCs (This prevents getting massive amounts of currency from things like Eater of Worlds)
  • ChainLightningMastery: Now only procs on Lightning damage as intended.
  • BurstingFuryMastery: Now only procs on Fire damage as intended.
  • ElectrostaticAttractionMastery: Fixed unintended stacks from non-lightning hits.
  • Resolves issue where temporary items disappear early.
  • Made Dexterity tooltip in the stats page accurate (0.1% use time modifier, not 1%)
  • Anticipated Strike now displays the correct amount of time between skills
  • Made Skillful Magic not hardcoded, match the tooltip length
  • Buffed Scorching Penance multiplier from 25% to 50%, clarified tooltip
  • Made mana regen consistently display on the stats screen
  • Fixed caught exception caused by recovery item logic check
  • Added Nova "basic" dust spam VFX to replace the broken old VFX
  • Made draggable elements (like the Passive Tree) not "jump" when clicking off the window and then back on it
  • Removed accidental Magic Guard functionality on Thunderboots
  • Adds missing damage scaling for IgniteDamage, this will fixed the Ignite Damage affix as well as the new Cinder Bastion keystone
  • Changes hard coded explosive slugs value
  • Adds missing % to the ChanceToApplyArmorShredAffix localization
  • Traps not being reset correctly on subworlds unloading
  • Various issues around swamp subworld loading and saving spawn point incorrectly
  • Various fixes around persisting boss downed state to prevent abuse of killing boss over and over without additional maps being used
  • Various fixes around the Goblin Surveyor System
    • Better spawning checks
    • Removed the accidental early spawn paths: the "Start" step force-spawn and the always-true life crystal gate.
    • Added server-side cleanup so any stale Surveyor that exists before the proper quest step despawns instead of hanging around.
  • Fixes Pyralis spawning issues in Dedicated Clients
