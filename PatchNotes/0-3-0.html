Conflux League Mechanic

 

Redesigned Passive Tree

The passive tree has been redesigned from the ground up. Now with hundreds of choices, allowing you to have unique choices all the way to level 100.

We will continue to evolve this as well as feedback comes in and more passives become possible.

The below list includes all new passives added in the redesigned passive tree.

  • Added lightning resistance
  • Added fire resistance
  • Added cold resistance
  • Added all elemental resistances
  • Increased magic & summon damage hybrid node
  • % Increased global attack speed

 

Bug Fixes

  • War shields' cooldowns will now tick at 66% rate when swapped to another item.
  • War shields will now player a very quiet sound when their cooldown period is over, making them easier to time, especially when swapped to another item such as the umbrella.
  • Ravencrest's portal structure will now generate within the middle third of the world, i.e. closer to spawn.
  • Fixed chargeable bows using up arrows even when not firing.
  • Fixed right-clicking a shield or another accessory with a designated slot being able to equip it into a generic accessory slot.
  • Fixed war shields' dash being triggered when using a different item and swapping to a war shield, sometimes allowing uncontrolled flight.
  • Fixed button tooltips not showing up in main menu.
  • Ravencrest Entrance Spawn should be prioritized more to home
