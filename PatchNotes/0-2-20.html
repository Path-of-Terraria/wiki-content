Elemental Damage System Overhaul

  • Changes elemental damage affixes to be exclusive to weapons
  • Added elemental damage to the appropriate vanilla items (Fire, Cold, Lightning, Chaos)
  • Reworked all ailments.
    • Ignite - A high damage fire damage DoT that scales with your damage, and is guaranteed on critical strikes. One can be active on a target by default. Any fire damage critical can inflict ignite.
    • Shock - A debuff that applies a damage multiplier to all incoming damage on that enemy. Your hits and shock effectiveness will scale this multiplier. Any lightning damage can inflict shock.
    • Freeze - A debuff that will “freeze” an enemy in place. Duration scales with damage, max enemy hp, and any “freeze duration” modifiers. Any cold damage critical can inflict freeze.
    • Poison - An infinitely stacking DoT that deals steady, ramping chaos damage over time. Chaos damage does NOT inflict poison by default. Specific skills/affixes/weapons can allow poison infliction
    • Bleed - A high damage DoT with limited stacking. Encourages aggressive playstyles to keep stacks up due to quick debuff drop-off rates. This affix can roll on melee & ranged weapons by default.
  • Fixed Shock multiplier not working for projectiles
  • Added "Chance to apply Bleed on hit", "Increased max Bleed stacks", and "More damage used for Bleed" item affixes that can roll on melee or ranged weapons only.
  • Added frozen effect for NPCs, shader and peripherals
  • Added freezing functionality to Cold damage, only on crits and doesn't work on bosses
  • Fixed "healing on killing a burning enemy" affix not applying for Ignited
  • Added conversion and resistance item affixes for Chaos
  • Added Chaotic mob affix
  • Replaced all vanilla Poison effects with a custom stacking Poison, dealing 4dps per stack
  • Stopped mob affix icons from drawing weirdly when the mob is frozen
  • Changed localizations for any "flat" to be "added" to stay consistent with flat being added bonuses.
  • Reworked tooltips to account for elemental damage. Damage numbers now have color based on their respective element(s).
  • Changes resistance affixes to only be rollable on equipment (non weapons) This ensures weapons are mostly exclusive to damage/utility affixes
