This is not yet released

  • Rewrote all hardmode maps to determine their area level/level condition by using the formula instead of hardcoding. This should result in less bugs around drops in maps not respecting the correct area level
  • Fixed Fishron Map dropping early
  • Fix for Skeletron Prime bag now dropping and throwing exceptions in odd scenarios
  • Stopped NPCs from dropping mech boss summons
  • War Shields and Boomerangs now have the right item level
  • Completed quests are now selectable to see the steps again
  • Completed quests can't be secretly pinned anymore
  • Fixed some mouse texts, such as that of the ruler mode and minimap hovers, not displaying.
  • Fixed tooltips not showing for item chat tags and similar mod elements
  • Dragged inventory items' tooltips will now also display when hovering over item chat tags.
  • Causing bosses to despawn in one way or another will no longer count as a victory. They will instead respawn in a few seconds.
  • Explorable maps now drop -1 to +1 of their tier. So in a tier 8 you can drop tier 7-9
  • Fixed passives outside of the initial viewport not responding due to existing outside of the parent UI element.
  • Fixes loot distribution in chests. Should match that of mob drops now
  • The implementation of extra accessory slots has been moved onto TML's shoulders, resolving all the remaining accessory-slot-related issues that were still present after the last batch of fixes.
  • Extra accessory slots added by other mods may now show up if not disallowed by Path Of Terraria's internal filtering.
  • Fixed various changes to stats not being applied when accessories are placed in additional slots (third or fourth). Examples: Shackles' defense, Pygmy Necklace.
  • Fixed right-click equipment logic behaving incorrectly when it came to extra accessory slots.
  • Fixed accessory dyes sometimes applying to the wrong accessories.
  • Players will now be able to retrieve items from extra dye and vanity slots that might have gotten 'stuck in the shadow realm' after the last batch of fixes.
  • Fixed staff hold animations desyncing in multiplayer.
  • Multiple rings of the same type can now be equipped at once.
  • Potions can now not be used while at full hp or full mp
  • Added sell price of 20silver to all grimoire items.
  • The check for “Tier x or Higher” explorable map completion was resolved. So if your quest is on “Tier 2” maps, you can complete 3,4, etc and it will count
  • Each tier of explorable map quest has been reduced from 10 → 5
  • Fixes an exception that could be thrown during the Elder Grovekeeper generation
