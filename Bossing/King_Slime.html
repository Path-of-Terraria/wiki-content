Getting to the King Slime Domain

 

Getting to the Domain

There are two ways to enter the King Slime's domain:

  1. Use a King Slime Map on a Map Device and enter, which is dropped by any NPC(1/1000 chance) after the Kill The King quest is completed
  2. After completing the first Class Quest on your world a new NPC named Garrick will appear in the tavern in Ravencrest. Talking to Garrick will task you with a quest to collect 5 Iron/Lead, 5 Gold/Platinum, and 1 Ruby to help build a Map Device. Bringing these materials back to Garrick will give you this device, as well as a map to enter with it. You can use this map with the Map Device to open a portal to the King's Domain.

Garrick's page will explain the quest, Kill the King.
