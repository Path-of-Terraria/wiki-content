Getting to the King Slime Domain

After completing the first class quest on your world a new NPC called Garrick  will appear in the tavern in Ravencrest.

Talking to Garrick he will provide you a map to the King Slime domain.

You can use this map with the Map Device to open a portal to the King's Domain.

Garrick's page will explain the quest, Kill the King.

 

Defeating King Slime

Once you defeat the King Slime you can now receive the King Slime Domain Map as a global drop.

This will allow you to fight through the King Slime Domain once again.
