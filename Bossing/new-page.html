How to start the quest to defeat her

The path to defeating the Queen Slime begins immediately after entering Hardmode.

To complete the quest, we need to:

  1. Explore 10 Tier 1 maps (IMPORTANT: only Tier 1).
  2. Enter the Queen Slime’s Lair.
  3. Defeat the Queen Slime.

This may be a bug, BUT you must specifically complete the “Forest” or “Desert” maps.

After completing the 10th map, the Queen Slime Map will drop into your inventory.
