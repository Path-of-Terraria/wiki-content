Getting to the Domain

There are two ways to enter the Eater of World's domain:

  1. Use an Eater of World's Map on a Map Device and enter, which is dropped by any NPC(1/1000 chance) after the Whispers of the Deep quest is completed
  2. Finding Morven in the underground corruption, freeing him, and progressing his questline, Whispers of the Deep. Once three Shadow Orb's have been destroyed, a portal will spawn, allowing you to challenge the Eater of Worlds.

Morven's page will explain the quest, Whispers of the Deep.
