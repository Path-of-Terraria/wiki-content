Fireball is a magic projectile skill that launches an explosive fireball.

Description: Cast a high-damage, explosive fireball which has a chance to burn enemies.

Requires: No weapon requirement.

Tags: Magic, Projectile, Fire, Area of Effect. Some specializations add Cold or Chaos.

Stats

Specializations

  • Inferno: Fireballs have a 100% chance to ignite enemies.
  • Frostfire Meteors: Fireballs are replaced with channeled meteors, which deal Cold and Fire damage.
  • Shadowflame Pyre: The fireball flies in an arc and leaves a pyre of shadowflame, dealing Chaos and Fire damage and inflicting Eternal Shadowflame.
