Nova is a magic area skill that creates a shockwave around the player.

Description: Create a shockwave around you based on your held magic weapon's damage.

Requires: A magic weapon held.

Tags: Magic, Area of Effect. Specializations add Fire, Cold, or Lightning.

Stats

Specializations

  • Fire Burst: Create an aura of flame based on your held magic weapon's damage, inflicting On Fire!
  • Ice Wave: Create a wave of ice based on your held magic weapon's damage, inflicting Chilled.
  • Thunder Shock: Create a pulse of electricity based on your held magic weapon's damage, dealing a wider range of damage and inflicting Shocked.
