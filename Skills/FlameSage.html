Flame Sage is a summon skill that conjures a fiery sentry.

Description: Conjure a fiery sentry that can draw the attention of enemies. Only two sentries can be out at a time.

Requires: No weapon requirement.

Tags: Summon, Projectile, Fire, Sentry.

Stats

Specializations

  • Flamethrower: Conjure a fiery sentry that deals continuous damage to enemies in a cone.
  • Molten Sentinel: Conjure a fiery sentry that deals continuous damage to enemies in a radius.
  • Volatile Construct: Conjure a fiery sentry that explodes after a short time, dealing additional damage based on remaining life.
