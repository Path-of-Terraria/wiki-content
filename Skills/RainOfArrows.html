Rain of Arrows, displayed in game as Flurry, is a ranged skill that fires a burst of projectiles using the held ranged weapon and ammo.

Description: Shoot a flurry of projectiles based on your held ranged weapon towards the cursor after a short delay.

Requires: A non-consumable ranged weapon held, usable ammo, and a weapon that is not channeled or blacklisted.

Tags: Ranged, Projectile. Some passives and specializations add Chaos or Cold.

Stats

Specializations

  • Nature's Barrage: Tampered tips poison enemies.
  • Explosive Volley: Arrows reposition with an explosion in a much wider range.
  • Piercing Precision: Replace the flurry with a delayed barrage with increased armor piercing.
