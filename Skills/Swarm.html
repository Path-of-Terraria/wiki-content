Pest Swarm is a summon skill that creates short-lived creatures to attack nearby enemies.

Description: Summon a short-lived creature to fight for you, which targets enemies automatically.

Requires: A summon weapon held.

Tags: Summon, Projectile. Some passives and specializations add Cold, Fire, or Lightning.

Stats

Specializations

  • Airborne Antlions: Halve the maximum amount of insects, but the insects fly and deal more damage.
  • Locust Brood: Twice the amount of insects spawn as eggs all at once, spawning after some time.
  • Glacial Antlions: Antlions deal 100% cold damage.
