Fetid Carapace is a ranged defensive projectile skill unlocked through the Fetid Carapace item affix.

Description: Gain some orbital projectiles that protect you from enemies and their projectiles.

Requires: A ranged weapon held and at least one Fetid Carapace affix level.

Tags: Projectile.

Stats

Using the skill again while the chunks are active sends them toward the cursor.
