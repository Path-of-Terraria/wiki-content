Path of Terraria Releases

 

0.2.0 (Unreleased)

This release marks the first public alpha release. We are hoping this to be the start of our growing community and bring in new fresh ideas to the mod.

  • Skill system overhaul - There is too much to list in detail. If you'd like to learn more check out https://github.com/Path-of-Terraria/PathOfTerraria/issues/764
    • In a nutshell there is now skill augmentations, skill enhancements, and a new item called Skill Augmentation Orb to unlock these slots.
    • It should create a system that is a hybrid between something like Last Epoch and Path of Exile
    • Some highlights are
      • 6 Skill Augmentations
      • Entirely redefined skill trees
      • New ways of thinking about skills
  • New Boss - Sun Devourer
    • Can be found at the end of the Desert Explorable Map
    • Includes a few new unique drops such as the Sunsoul Staff + More
    • Includes several new Desert Prefabs to be explored
  • New Boss Domain - Destroyer
    • Includes a completely reimagined map utilizing several small events and triggers for fighting the Destroyer
    • New enemies in the map
    • New items to be found in the map
    • Dropped upon completion of 10th Tier 4+ Map
  • New Boss Domain - Plantera
    • Adds Budding Plantera miniboss
    • Contains various jungle related enemies
    • Dropped upon completion of 10th Tier 5+ Map
  • New Boss Domain - Golem
    • Dropped upon completion of 10th Tier 6+ Map
  • New Boss Domain - Fishron
    • Dropped upon completion of 10th Tier 7+ Map
  • New Boss Domain - Empress of Light
    • Dropped upon completion of 10th Tier 8+ Map
    • Includes 2 brand new enemies to encounter
  • New Boss Domain - Lunatic Cultist
    • Is the domain that leads up to the Moonlord Fight.
    • Dropped upon completion of 10th Tier 9+ Map
  • New Boss Domain - Moon Lord
    • The most involved boss domain to date.
    • Several new tiles and areas to explore.
    • Continued on from the Lunatic Cultist domain
  • New Game / Damage Mechanic - Damage Types + Resistances
    • There are now 3 new types of damage that can be applied - Cold, Lightning, and Fire
      • This means that a enemy can deal “Base” damage (vanilla damage) - And elemental damage (One of the three above)
      • Enemies can convert some of their base damage, or have additional elemental damage added to their base damage.
    • You can now obtain Lightning Resistance , Fire Resistance , and Cold Resistance on gear.
    • Damage types have a chance to apply a relevant debuff to the target hit. View the Damage Types Wiki Page for more information
      • Fire → On Fire
      • Cold → Chilled
      • Lightning → Shocked
    • Some enemy mobs now deal relevant damage of that type
      • Hellbat → Fire
      • Ice Slime → Cold
      • Ice Elemental → Lightning
    • Blue Slime has a new “Electric" Variant that deals Lightning Damage
    • Mob Affixes have been added so Magic & Rare mobs can spawn with Cold/Ice/Fire Enchanted Mob Affixes
    • Adds in fire, cold and lightning resistance affixes to all non-map, non-jewel items

    • Once you get to the tiered Explorable Maps you will begin progressing towards hardmode bosses. The first one you will encounter is Queen Bee and this can be gotten to by clearing 10 of the tier (or higher) required for that boss.
    • You will not start progressing towards the next higher tier boss until you defeat the boss for the lower tier.
    • Queen Bee Domain Added (Tier 1+) Map Completions
    • Twins Boss Domain Added (Tier 2+) Map Completions
  • NEW - Guided Tour Quest
    • When you first arrive inside the Path of Terraria you will be taken on a guided tour of it's features and go over some of the mechanics available in the mod.
    • This should allow new players an easier entry into the mod to learn more about it and get them going.
  • Fixes an issue where non-hostile or critter mobs were getting monster modifiers.
  • Added a tool's power to the tooltip display for any in-game tools.
  • Fixes an issue where holding a staff on a mount would look weird
  • Added an image for the mod on the Steam Workshop
  • Passive tree was updated with a few new passives for attributes. Additionally this added a new system which allows us to have different values for the same passive throughout the tree allowing for more combinations.

 

0.1.13 - February 6th, 2025

  • NEW - Enemy Modifier Mechanics
    • Enemies that have a rarity will now spawn with random modifiers
    • Modifiers that are effecting a mob will be displayed above the mob
    • Magic Enemies will spawn with 1 affix
    • Rare enemies will spawn with 2 affixes
    • There will be additional content in-game that could add/remove/modify these affixes in the future to increase these limits and spawning methods.
    • There are currently 4 modifiers
      • Bulky
      • Fast
      • Aggravator
      • Siphoner
      • Harmful
  • Garrick now has a custom sprite - Previously using Barkeep.
  • New Item - Warden Bow
  • New Item - Rooted Amulet
  • New Debuff - Rooted
    • Locks enemy in place for 1 second.
    • Bosses cannot be rooted.
    • Not in the general pool of affixes and only provided through unique items.
  • Fixes an issue that caused implicit modifiers to not appear on items sometimes.
  • The Skills Hotkey display has been updated to only show 1 character but you can now hover the hotkey to see more information about the skill and full hotkey name.
  • Minera, Ash Wraith, and Fire Maw now all have banners
  • Health and mana potions removed from Bestiary to clean that up some
  • Fixes KeyNotFoundException being thrown by a Clothier's checking if a quest is available when you load into a world with him nearby
  • New Endgame Mechanic - Mapping
    • Maps have 11 tiers. You can start to drop Tier 1 maps upon reaching Hardmode.
    • Maps will have “Themes” which will dictate what type of mobs you encounter and what bosses you may encounter to complete the map.
    • Full Multiplayer Support
    • Adds in the Map Modifier System.
      • You can now roll your maps Magic/Rare using the in-game currency to increase the difficult.
      • The rewards within them will be greater. Including quantity of items and rarity of items
    • Adds the Forest Map
      • The Forest Map has a unique new boss. Elder Grovekeeper
      • New Mob Type - Ent
      • New Mob Type - Entling
      • New Mob Type - Canopy Bird
  • Queen Bee Domain is now considered the Jungle
  • Fixes an issue with the Map Device not consuming items correctly
  • Improves grass growing in Eye of Cthulu Domain
  • Fixes a few edge cases for Deerclops Domain for generation
  • Deerclops domain lighting has been improved
  • Hides quest icons from displaying when in undiscovered areas
  • Stops the Old Man's icon from rendering & showing hover text when not discovered, to hide the Dungeon before discovery
  • Removes all Hardmode natural boss spawns. This content will be replaced with the Boss Domains
  • Fixes King Slime and Eye of Cthulu being able to be naturally spawned. No longer spawns naturally.

  • NEW - Global Trade System
    • Trade with other players playing Path of Terraria - Even if they aren't on your server or in your world.
    • You can use https://pathofterraria.com/trade to see trades other players have created. You can also use the command /bui to open the buy UI.
    • When you click buy on an item on the website you will send a webhook directly to your TML client to open the Buy UI for that item allowing you to complete the trade.
    • To list an item you can use /suito open the sell UI to list your item.
    • Trades are completed with the in-game currency shards. These currency shards are apart of the global drop table.
  • Adds in GUID tracking for players instead of by name. Should allow for renaming your players.
