Affixes

Affixes can be considered to how accessories in vanilla terraria can have a stat on them. The amount of affixes is based on the Gear Rarity. The types of affixes that can appear on items depend on the gear itself.

 

Rarity

Gear has 4 different rarity types

  • Common
  • Magic (Up to 2 Affixes)
  • Rare (Up to 4 Affixes)
  • Unique (Set Affixes)
