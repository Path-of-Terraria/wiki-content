The ent is a larger adult version of the Entling.

 

Stats

 

 

Behavior

The ent is a slow moving large target. The Ent has two forms of attacks. A ranged thorn attack and a melee swing attack.

The ranged thorn attack happens randomly between 1 and 3 seconds while traveling towards the player slowly.

When the Ent is in melee range it will swing it's large branch-like arms at you
