The Elder Grovekeeper is a boss that has a chance of spawning within Forest Explorer Maps in the Boss Arena

 

Appearance

A towering humanoid tree creature, its face a hollowed-out glowing knot. Its limbs are gnarled and covered in moss, with glowing runes etched across its bark. The Grovekeeper is equipped with a gnarled staff which resembles bark twisted together like vines

 

Arena 

The Grovekeeper's Arena is an overgrown ancient ritualist site where old large stones are covered in moss and vines that seem to have some lingering magic within them still.

 

Behavior

The Grovekeeper doesn't move around very much but does interact with the environment of the arena quite a bit.

 

Stone Throws

The most common attack the Grovekeeper will perform is throwing large boulders at you. Dealing massive contact damage if not dodged.

 

Ancient Runic Magic

The Grovekeeper will wave it's gnarled staff in the air activating the old runic large stones in the arena which will burst in a circle of magic around them after 1 second of charging.

 

Loot

Rooted Amulet - A unique (undetermined base) amulet that has a chance to root enemies in place when hit.

Warden's Bow - A unique (undetermined base) bow that's arrows split on impact shooting thorns out at the point of impact.
