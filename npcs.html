In Path of Terraria you will encounter several new NPCs that will appear in towns, random events, and more.

NPCs will give out quests and guide you on your Path of Terraria playthrough. 

 

List of NPCs

Class NPCs

These NPCs are all found naturally in Ravencrest, and specialize in a class.

Thrain Ironfist (Melee)

Elara (Ranged)

Alaric (Magic)

Morgana (Summoning)

 

Quest NPCs

These NPCs are not found in Ravencrest, but directly relate to one or more quests.

Eldric, The Moon Watcher

Morven, the Mad Scientist

 

Traveling NPCs

These NPCs can be found in Ravencrest, sometimes offering quests, other times just talking to other townspeople.

Garrick

Lloyd

Rhine

 

Vanilla NPCs

The Old Man
