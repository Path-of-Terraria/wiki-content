Questing is the main form of progression in Path of Terraria. Through quests you will unlock new skills, new areas, fight bosses, and discover all the new things in Path of Terraria.

Most, but not all, quests are found by talking to an NPC and clicking the “Quest” button. 

Class Quests

Class quests are quests that are specific to a specific class archetype (Melee, Magic, Ranged, Summoner) and unlock learning new skills and abilities for those classes.

Each quest is provided by a town NPC in Ravencrest. 

Thrain Ironfist: Melee - Forging a New Blade
Elara: Ranged - Drawing the Bow
Alaric: Magic - Magical Returnal
Morgana: Summoner - The Ancient Art
