Questing is the main form of progression in Path of Terraria. Through quests you will unlock new skills, new areas, and discover all the new things in Path of Terraria

 

Class Quests

Class quests are quests that are specific to a specific class archetype (Melee, Magic, Ranged, Summoner) and unlock learning new skills and abilities for those classes.
