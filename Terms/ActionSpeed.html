Understanding Action Speed will help you to build your characters.

Other terms to know

  • Movement Speed
  • Cooldown
  • Cast Speed

 

Definition

Action speed is the combined speed of all actions in Terraria + Path of Terraria. Action speed includes any action that the character, or monster, is performing. 

This includes the following

  • Cast Speed of Spells (Applies to any skills that have an explicit cast time)
  • Swinging a weapon (Decreases Use Time - Allowing for more swings/minute)
  • Firing a Ranged Weapon (Draw bow speeds, shot delay)
  • Movement Speed of Summons
  • Movement Speed

 

This does not include

  • Cooldowns
