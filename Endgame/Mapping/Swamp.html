The Swamp Map is a type of Explorer Map with the theme of Swamp. In the extracted subworld data, the area itself is named Mossbound Swamp.

 

The Swamp was introduced in 0.3.0 as the first exploration map built around the custom encounter system. In addition to regular off-screen spawns, the area can trigger encounter-based fights as you move through the map.

 

Traversal and Layout

The subworld description for Mossbound Swamp highlights three major traversal elements:

  • Jumping across floating objects
  • Swimming through hostile insect-infested sections
  • Exploring overgrown treetops on the way to the boss

Bosses

The Swamp boss is Younger Caracarcoss, whose internal NPC name is Mossmother. This boss is also one of the explorable-map completion checks referenced by Distant Travels.

 

Notes

  • The Swamp map item's tooltip flavor text is: The map smells of moss and wood.
  • Patch Notes 0.3.0 also mention Swamp Water and a new enemy grabbing system as part of the Swamp update.
