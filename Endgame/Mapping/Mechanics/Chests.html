Chests are one of the main loot sources found while exploring maps.

 

They are usually tied to points of interest, side areas, or hidden structures rather than the final boss encounter itself.

 

Where Chests Show Up

Current map pages show chests appearing inside points of interest. For example, the Forest Map page explicitly calls out:

  • Ancient Trees, which contain a chest in the canopy
  • Hidden Caves, which hide a chest alongside traps

 

This makes chests part of the reward for fully exploring a map rather than only rushing to the boss.

 

How Chest Loot Fits Into Mapping

  • Chests reward players for checking side paths and structures.
  • They are part of the map's optional loot content.
  • Chest loot complements boss rewards and random drops from enemies.

 

Virtual Bag Interaction

If the Virtual Loot Bag is enabled, gear looted during an explorable map is redirected into that bag instead of staying in your inventory immediately. The bag opens when you leave the map and clears between map runs.

 

This does not replace chests themselves, but it changes how map loot is reviewed after you collect it.
