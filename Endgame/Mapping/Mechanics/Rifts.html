Rifts are optional encounter content that can appear inside eligible explorable maps.

 

They are part of the Conflux League systems and add extra combat encounters, extra rewards, and another reason to fully explore a map.

 

How Rifts Work

  • Rifts can spawn inside eligible mapping subworlds.
  • They are activated by right-clicking them.
  • Once activated, they begin a timed combat encounter with themed enemy waves.
  • Completing more of the encounter increases Rift Stability and improves rewards.

 

What They Reward

Standard rifts reward Conflux, a map resource stored in the world's Map Device resource pool rather than your normal inventory.

  • Infernal Rift -> Infernal Conflux
  • Glacial Rift -> Glacial Conflux
  • Celestial Rift -> Celestial Conflux

 

Why They Matter In Maps

  • They are optional side encounters inside a map run.
  • Higher-tier maps can spawn more rifts.
  • They provide extra rewards beyond ordinary mob drops, chests, and the map boss.
