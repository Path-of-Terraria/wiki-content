The Forest area is an explorable map unlocked as soon as you beat the Wall of Flesh. It contains many new enemies, a new boss, and some new loot, alongside continuing hardmode's map progression.

 

Points of Interest

 

Abandoned Campsite

Abandoned Campsites are purely aesthetic, adding variety to the Forest. They, as with all other points of interest in the Forest, have 3 variants.

Ancient Tree

Ancient Trees are large, hallowed trees with a small area in their canopy, holding a chest full of loot.

Glade Shrine

Glade Shrines are large, tree-like shrines with water features, presumably used by current or former Forest dwellers for some purpose. They are purely decorative.

Hidden Cave

The rarest of the points of interest, these caves hide a chest with loot in them alongside a few traps.
