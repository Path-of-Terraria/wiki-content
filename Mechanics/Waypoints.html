Waypoints are how someone can traverse the vast world of Path of Terraria quickly. Waypoints have no cooldown and you can use it as often as you'd like.

 

Unlocking

Waypoints are unlocked as you visit certain areas around the terraria world. These often include locations to certain places, events, or towns you encounter.

 

Using Waypoints

Using the Arcane Obelisk you can teleport to unlocked areas with ease.
