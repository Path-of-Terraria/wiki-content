Attributes are core character stats in Path of Terraria. There are 3 attributes in total:

  • Strength
  • Dexterity
  • Intelligence

 

You can view your current totals in the character information panel under the Attributes section.

Attributes are currently gained from two main sources:

  • The passive tree, including single-attribute and hybrid attribute nodes
  • Item affixes found on gear

 

What each attribute does

Attribute bonuses are applied in steps of 10 points. Partial points still count toward your total, but the base stat bonus only increases once you reach the next 10-point breakpoint.

 

Strength

Strength increases your maximum life.

Every 10 Strength grants +5 maximum life.

Examples:

  • 10 Strength = +5 maximum life
  • 25 Strength = +10 maximum life
  • 30 Strength = +15 maximum life

 

Dexterity

Dexterity increases your global attack speed.

Every 10 Dexterity grants +1% attack speed.

Unlike Strength and Intelligence, this bonus scales directly with your total Dexterity instead of only at full 10-point breakpoints.

 

Intelligence

Intelligence increases your maximum mana.

Every 10 Intelligence grants +5 maximum mana.

Examples:

  • 10 Intelligence = +5 maximum mana
  • 25 Intelligence = +10 maximum mana
  • 30 Intelligence = +15 maximum mana

 

How to obtain attributes

Passive tree

The passive tree contains dedicated attribute nodes for all three attributes. It also contains hybrid nodes that grant two attributes at once:

  • Intelligence + Strength
  • Intelligence + Dexterity
  • Dexterity + Strength

These nodes are a reliable way to build toward the stat mix your character needs.

 

Item affixes

Attributes can also roll as item affixes on gear.

  • Strength can roll from 3 to 37 per affix tier
  • Dexterity can roll from 3 to 37 per affix tier
  • Intelligence can roll from 3 to 37 per affix tier

These affixes begin appearing at minimum level 1 and improve at higher levels, with stronger tiers unlocking at levels 9, 18, 25, 34, and 45.

Attribute affixes are available across most non-map equipment and jewels, with shields favoring Strength, quivers favoring Dexterity, and talismans favoring Intelligence.
