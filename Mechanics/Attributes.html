In Path of Terraria there are 3 attributes in total that can be obtained.

  • Strength
  • Dexterity
  • Intelligence

 

Attributes can be obtained through Gear, or the Passive Tree System.

You can view how much you have of each of these attributes in the character information panel.

Certain pieces of gear might have a requirement to them which must be met or exceeded before being able to equip the item

 

Strength

+5 Life Per 10 Points

 

Dexterity

+1% Attack Speed Per 10 Points

 

Intelligence

+5 Mana Per 10 Points
