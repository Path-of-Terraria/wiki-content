Heart of the Machine is the Tinkerer quest for The Destroyer.

 

Prerequisites

  • Twin Terrors must be completed.
  • The Twins must already be defeated.
  • You must re-enter Ravencrest after completing Twin Terrors.

 

Steps

  1. Talk to the Tinkerer to start the quest.
  2. Complete both of the following:
    Collect 20 Cursed Flames or Ichor
    Defeat 5 Clingers or Ichor Stickers
  3. Return to the Tinkerer. He gives you a Destroyer Map.
  4. Enter the Destroyer's domain.
  5. Defeat The Destroyer.
  6. Return to the Tinkerer to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience
