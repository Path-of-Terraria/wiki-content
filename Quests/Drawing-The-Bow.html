Description

The intro class quest for the Ranged class. Elara the Hunter gives this quest, and can be found in her hut on the left side of Ravencrest.

Prerequisites

There are no prerequisites for this quest, and can be completed at the beginning of your playthrough.

Steps

Step 1: Talk to Elara

Elara: “The attack left quite a mess… half my supplies are scattered or ruined. I need to figure out what survived. Would you mind gathering a few things so I can start repairs?”

She asks for you to retrieve the following:

Silk: 20
Wood: 50
Stone Blocks: 50

Step 2: Return to Elara with the materials

Elara: “Perfect. This should let me patch up most of the lodge. Come back soon — it should look much better by then.”

Then she asks another favor:

Elara: “One more thing. The townsfolk keep hearing wings at night. If you could thin out the flying monsters around here, everyone would rest a lot easier. And… I still need a few more materials after that.”

She asks for the following:

Wood: 40
Gel: 10
Iron or Lead Bars: 20

Step 3: Kill 10 Airborne Enemies

(Demon Eyes, Crimera, or Eater of Souls)

Step 4: Return to Elara with the materials

Elara: “The lodge will be fully repaired next time you come by. You’ve done more than enough — really. Here, let me teach you something passed down from hunters before me… the Rain of Arrows technique.”

Rewards


Rain of Arrows Skill: Learned
