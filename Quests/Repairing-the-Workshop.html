Repairing the Workshop is the opening hardmode quest and introduces the Tinkerer quest line.

The quest is given by the Tinkerer once Hardmode is active.

 

Prerequisites

  • Hardmode must be active.

 

Steps

  1. Talk to the Tinkerer and provide:
    100 Wood
    20 Wire
    50 Stone Blocks
    1 Teleporter
  2. The workshop is upgraded as part of the quest progression.
  3. Talk to the Tinkerer again to finish the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience

 

Notes

  • This quest must be completed before the later Tinkerer hardmode boss quests become available.
