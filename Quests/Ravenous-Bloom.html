Ravenous Bloom is Morgana's hardmode quest for Plantera.

 

Prerequisites

  • The Prime Reconstruction must be completed.
  • All three mechanical bosses must be defeated.

 

Steps

  1. Complete both of the following:
    Give Morgana 5 Vines and 10 Jungle Spores
    Defeat 10 Jungle Bats
  2. Talk to Morgana. She gives you a Plantera Map.
  3. Enter Plantera's domain.
  4. Defeat Plantera.
  5. Return to Morgana to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience

 

Notes

  • Some of this quest's objectives are marked as temporary placeholders in the source code and are expected to be replaced with custom jungle content later.
