Holy Absolution is Alaric's hardmode quest for the Empress of Light.

 

Prerequisites

  • Mad Pig...ron must be completed.
  • Duke Fishron must already be defeated.

 

Steps

  1. Collect 10 Prism Shards.
  2. Talk to Alaric and give him the 10 Prism Shards. He gives you an Empress of Light Map.
  3. Enter the Hallow Rift.
  4. Defeat the Empress of Light.
  5. Return to Alaric to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience
