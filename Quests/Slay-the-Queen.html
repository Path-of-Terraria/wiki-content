Slay the Queen is Garrick's first hardmode boss quest and leads to the Queen Slime domain.

 

Prerequisites

  • Hardmode must be active.

 

Steps

  1. Talk to Garrick to start the quest.
  2. Complete both of the following:
    Collect 5 Souls of Light
    Defeat 10 Illuminant Slimes
  3. Return to Garrick. He gives you a Queen Slime Map.
  4. Enter the Queen Slime domain.
  5. Defeat Queen Slime.
  6. Return to Garrick and give him 1 Royal Jelly Core to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience
