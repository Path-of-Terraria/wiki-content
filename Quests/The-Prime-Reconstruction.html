The Prime Reconstruction is the Tinkerer quest for Skeletron Prime.

 

Prerequisites

  • Heart of the Machine must be completed.
  • The Destroyer must already be defeated.
  • You must re-enter Ravencrest after completing Heart of the Machine.

 

Steps

  1. Talk to the Tinkerer and provide:
    10 Souls of Night
    20 Bones
    5 Wood
  2. Talk to the Tinkerer again. He gives you a Prime Map.
  3. Enter Skeletron Prime's domain.
  4. Defeat Skeletron Prime.
  5. Return to the Tinkerer to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience

 

Notes

  • The current `5 Wood` hand-in is marked as a placeholder in the source code and is expected to be replaced later.
