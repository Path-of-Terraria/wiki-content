Distant Travels is the current epilogue quest that follows World's End.

 

Prerequisites

  • World's End must be completed.
  • Moon Lord must already be defeated.

 

Steps

  1. Defeat any Explorable Domain boss. The current code checks for bosses such as the Sun Devourer, Grovetender, or Mossmother.
  2. Talk to Azariel to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 100,000 experience
