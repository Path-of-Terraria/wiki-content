Ancient Design is Thrain's hardmode quest for Golem.

 

Prerequisites

  • Ravenous Bloom must be completed.
  • Plantera must already be defeated.

 

Steps

  1. Defeat 10 Lihzahrds or Lihzahrd Crawlers.
  2. Give Thrain 2 Lihzahrd Power Cells.
  3. Talk to Thrain. He gives you a Golem Map.
  4. Enter Golem's domain.
  5. Defeat Golem.
  6. Return to Thrain to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience

 

Notes

  • The code comments indicate this quest will likely get a more bespoke temple interaction later.
