Twin Terrors is a Tinkerer quest that unlocks the domain for The Twins.

 

Prerequisites

  • Repairing the Workshop must be completed.
  • Queen Slime must already be defeated.
  • You must re-enter Ravencrest after completing the previous Tinkerer quest.

 

Steps

  1. Talk to the Tinkerer to begin.
  2. Complete all of the following:
    Collect 5 Lunar Fragments
    Defeat 3 Wraiths
    Defeat 3 Possessed Armors
    Defeat 3 Wandering Eyes
  3. Return to the Tinkerer. He gives you a Twins Map.
  4. Enter the Twins domain.
  5. Defeat both Retinazer and Spazmatism.
  6. Return to the Tinkerer to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience

 

Notes

  • Lunar Fragments are enabled as a quest-specific drop during this quest.
