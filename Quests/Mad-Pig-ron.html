Mad Pig...ron is the Fisherman's hardmode quest for Duke Fishron.

 

Prerequisites

  • Ancient Design must be completed.
  • Golem must already be defeated.

 

Steps

  1. Complete both of the following:
    Collect 10 Coral
    Defeat 10 Ocean enemies such as Sharks, Crabs, or Jellyfish
  2. Talk to the Fisherman.
  3. Return to the Fisherman and give him 1 Truffle Worm. He gives you a Fishron Map.
  4. Enter Duke Fishron's domain.
  5. Defeat Duke Fishron.
  6. Return to the Fisherman to complete the quest.

 

Rewards

  • 30,000 experience

 

Notes

  • The code also includes an underwater rift system for this quest, but the current quest flow still grants a Fishron Map directly.
  • Some enemy requirements are marked as temporary in the source and may change to custom post-Golem ocean enemies later.
