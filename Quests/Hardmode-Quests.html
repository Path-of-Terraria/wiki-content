The hardmode quest line begins once the Wall of Flesh has been defeated and Hardmode is active.

This quest chain covers the post-Wall boss progression through Queen Slime, the mechanical bosses, Plantera, Golem, Duke Fishron, Empress of Light, and the endgame sequence.

 

Quest Order

The currently implemented hardmode progression is:

  1. Repairing the Workshop
  2. Slay the Queen
  3. Twin Terrors
  4. Heart of the Machine
  5. The Prime Reconstruction
  6. Ravenous Bloom
  7. Ancient Design
  8. Mad Pig...ron
  9. Holy Absolution
  10. World's End
  11. Distant Travels

 

Notes

  • These pages reflect the current quest code in the mod source.
  • Several hardmode quests still contain placeholder or temporary objectives in code, and those are called out on the relevant pages.
  • Most hardmode boss quests currently reward experience and a boss map used to enter the corresponding domain.
