Description

The intro class quest for the Melee class. Thrain Ironfist gives this quest, and can be found in his Forge in Ravencrest.

Prerequisites

There are no prerequisites for this quest, and can be completed at the beginning of your playthrough.

Steps

Step 1: Talk to Thrain Ironfist

Thrain Ironfist: As you can see, some terrible event happened that simply obliterated my forge. What happened? I don't know, I sleep like a rock... anyhow. I'd love some help gathering materials, the town's metal has run dry for a while now. And maybe some wood while you're at it.

He asks for you to retrieve the following:

Lead or Iron Ore:20
Lead or Iron Hammer:1
Stone:50
Wood:20

Step 2:

Thrain Ironfist: “Fantastic! I'll fix the old forge up quick as I can. Here, take this sword. It's a test I made right before the attack. I'd like for you to test it on some zombies; those buggers are awful to deal with. If you'd be so kind, I'd also enjoy some more materials. Then I think we can get the Iron Anvil truly back to her feet!”

He asks for the following:

Stone Blocks:30
Wood:50
Gold or Platinum Bars:10

Step 3: Kill 15 Zombies

Step 4: Return to Thrain with the materials

Thrain Ironfist: “It almost brings a tear to the eye. The Iron Forge is almost ready for work! Here, this is a lesson I've got from my history in battle: Sometimes, you gotta go crazy. Just be careful! And come back whenever you wish — you've been a great help!”

Rewards


Experience:500
Magic Rarity Sword:1 (Random)
Magic Rarity Battleaxe:1 (Random)
