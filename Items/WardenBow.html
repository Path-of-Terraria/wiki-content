The Warden Bow is a boss drop from the Elder Grovekeeper and is not available on the global drop table.

The Warden Bow is based of the Wooden Bow

 

Properties

2-3% To Apply Rooted on Hit

25-35 Life

+10-20 Dexterity

+1 to Fetid Carapace Skill

 

Behavior

Projectiles fired will splinter on hit and shoot 3 thorn projectiles out from the point of impact in random directions.
