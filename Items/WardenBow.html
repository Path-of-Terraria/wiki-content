The Warden's Bow is a boss drop from the Elder Grovekeeper and is not available on the global drop table. The default fire mode works like any other Bow. The alt fire has the arrow deal significantly more damage, and split into 3 splinters on impact, dealing ~300% additional damage if they all hit.

The Warden's Bow is based of the Wooden Bow.

 

Properties

  • 2-3% To Apply Rooted on Hit
  • 25-35 Life
  • +10-20 Dexterity
  • +1 to Fetid Carapace Skill
