Prism Shards are hardmode quest items used for the Holy Absolution quest.

 

How to obtain Prism Shards

  • Prism Shards drop from the enemy Nostalgic Shard.
  • Nostalgic Shards can spawn in the Hallow after Duke Fishron has been defeated.
  • Outside the Empress of Light domain, Nostalgic Shards are only visible and hittable if you have Crystal Visors active.

 

Important note

When you start Holy Absolution, the Wizard gives you Crystal Visors. Equip them before farming in the overworld Hallow, otherwise the Nostalgic Shards will not be properly visible or targetable.

 

Alternative source

Nostalgic Shards also appear during the Empress of Light domain encounter, but for the quest itself most players will gather their 10 Prism Shards from Hallow farming after starting the quest.
