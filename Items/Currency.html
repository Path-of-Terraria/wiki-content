Currencies (Shards) are items in the game that can be used in the trade market and also can be used to modify your Gear. 

Types

  • Unfolding Shard
    • Upgrades a Normal item to Magic
  • Glimmering Shard
    • Rerolls the affixes on a Magic Item
  • Limpid Shard
    • Removes all affixes from a magic or rare item and turn it white
  • Radiant Shard
    • Rerolls the affix values of a magic, rare, or unique item
  • Ascendant Shard
    • Adds a new affix to an item that has an available affix slot
  • Echoing Shard
    • Creates a clone copy of a magic or rare item
  • Mystic Shard
    • Upgrades a Normal item to Rare
  • Shifting Shard
    • Rerolls the affixes on a Rare Item
  • Corrupt Shard
    • A currency shard that corrupts an item unpredictably.
