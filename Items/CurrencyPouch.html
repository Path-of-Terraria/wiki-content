The Currency Pouch is a built-in shard storage and crafting UI for Path of Terraria currencies.

It is designed for Currency Shards. When you pick up a supported shard, it is stored in the pouch automatically instead of going into your normal inventory.

 

Opening the Currency Pouch

  • Press P by default to toggle the Currency Pouch.
  • You can also click the Currency Pouch button while your inventory is open.

 

What it stores

The pouch stores shard-type currencies. The current pouch UI supports these shard slots:

  • Unfolding Shard
  • Glimmering Shard
  • Mystic Shard
  • Limpid Shard
  • Corrupt Shard
  • Ascendant Shard
  • Shifting Shard
  • Severance Shard
  • Radiant Shard
  • Echoing Shard

Each shard type is stored separately, and each type can hold up to that shard's normal maximum stack size.

 

Automatic storage

  • Picking up a supported shard sends it straight into the pouch.
  • You can also Shift + Left Click a shard stack in your inventory to store it in the pouch.
  • Inside the pouch UI, Shift + Left Click on a shard icon stores every matching shard from your inventory.

 

Taking shards back out

  • Ctrl + Left Click on a shard icon in the pouch takes out 1 shard.
  • Ctrl + Shift + Left Click on a shard icon takes out as many as your inventory can hold.

 

Applying shards from the pouch

The pouch includes a dedicated item slot for gear modification.

  • Place the item you want to modify into the slot near the bottom of the pouch UI.
  • Right Click a shard icon in the pouch to apply that shard directly to the slotted item.
  • If the shard cannot be used on the slotted item, the pouch tooltip shows the failure reason instead.

 

Notes

  • The Currency Pouch is separate from your normal inventory.
  • It only stores supported shard currencies, not every special item used by crafting or progression.
  • For the list of shard currencies themselves, see Currency.
