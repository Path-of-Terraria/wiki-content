The Arcane Obelisk is a placeable object that you can put anywhere in your world. Only one Arcane Obelisk may be placed in the overworld. This will unlock being able to travel to unlocked Waypoints

 

Obtaining

The Arcane Obelisk can be crafted using the following materials

  • 1x Diamond
  • 1x Emerald
  • 1x Ruby
  • 20 Iron or Lead Bars
