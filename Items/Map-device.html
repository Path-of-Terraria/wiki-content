The Map Device is an item that is the main way to progress through Path of Terraria. It allows you to travel to other â€œdomains", and kill that domain's specific boss. 

This item is given to you upon completing the first step of Garrick's quest, Kill the King, which requires the player to bring 5 Iron/Lead Bars, 5 Gold/Platinum Bars, and 1 Ruby to him.

The item can also be crafted, and has the below recipe.

  • 5 Iron/Lead Bars
  • 5 Gold/Platinum Bars
  • 1 Ruby
