The Old Man is a vanilla NPC, found at the steps of the Dungeon as soon as you get there. After beating the Eye of Cthulhu, he offers the Skeletron quest.

 

Boss Domain Quest - Skeletal Remnants

Start: Talk to the Old Man

The Old Man talks about how, while he can't remember much, he knows two things: terrible stuff is happening under him, and that he had talked to a man - short, bearded - in a nearby town some time somewhat recently. That's the only lead he gives.

Next: Talk to Thrain Ironfist 

Thrain recalls a man in a red outfit passing by. He mentioned a few things while he was there:

  1. 2 candles
  2. 1 “book of evil”
  3. 1 “evil bar”

He also recalls that the red man said something about “evil powder on a book”, though he's not entirely sure what that meant.

Throwing a vile or vicious powder on a book tile will turn it into Ancient Evil Book 

Next: Return to the Old Man and show him 1 Ancient Evil Book, 2 Candles/Platinum Candles, and 1 Demonite/Crimtane Bar 

Possessed by the curse that binds him, yet strikingly clear, the Old Man recites two poems:

    A light at each side;
One bar of evil below,
A book sits, starting.

    Many skulls screaming,
On the bricks of the prison.
A portal appears.

The Ancient Evil Book also has a diagram mimicking this poem in case the player forgets.

Both describe the ritual which would open a portal to the domain of Skeletron, which looks like this:

Next: Enter Skeletron's domain

Skeletron's domain is large and trecherous. Unlike others, the player must explore the dungeon in its entirety, crawling with traps, spikes and other dangers, in order to progress to the dwelling place of Skeletron itself.

There are three floors - each floor having one more room than the last. Each room must be completed in order to progress.

Each room has a series of obstacles, some much more complex than others, ending in a lever. All levers must be flipped on before continuing.

Next: Kill Skeletron

Next: Return to the Clothier

The Clothier mentions how just a few moments ago, he turned from the Old Man back to his usual self. He thanks you, and says he has things to think about. The quest ends.

Rewards

  • 10000 Experience
  • The Skeletron boss domain map can now be dropped as a global item (1/1000)
  • A rare accessory (Amulet or Ring)
