Eldric - The Moon Watcher

Eldric is an astronomer specializing in celestial phenomena and omens. He noticed the Eye of Cthulhu stirring in the night skies and wants the player’s help to prevent impending danger. 

Appearance: Eldric has robes adorned with star patterns, carrying a staff topped with a moon crystal.

 

Location

Eldric can be found next to his tent in the Desert after defeating the King Slime Boss Domain

 

Eye of Cthulhu Domain - The Rising Omen

Eldric notices the player's involvement with defeating King Slime and offers to share his concerns about the Eye of Cthulhu. This quest should only become available after the King Slime questline is completed.

Starting: Speak to Eldric in the Desert.

Eldric speaks of strange occurrences—distorted starlight, whispers in the night, and the moon crying an unnatural red. He believes an ancient evil, the Eye of Cthulhu, is awakening.

Eldric sends the player to collect 5 Lunar Shards, rare fragments found on wandering enemies at night, symbolizing the growing power of the Eye. This provides a thematic reason for encountering foes and getting accustomed to night exploration.

Eldric mentions that the Lunar Shards can be used to create a liquid for a ritual that can take place during night to bring out the Eye of Cthulhu

Lunar shards drop from Demon Eyes , and Zombies during the night time. They have a 1 in 5 drop rate.

Next: Bring the 5 Lunar Shards to Garrick

Garrick hands you a “Strange Liquid". He mentions that the liquid should be ready to be mixed with the lunar shards to summon the Eye of Cthulhu

Next: Return to Eldric

Eldric mentions he is ready to make the liquid and start the ritual. At this point a confirmation is sent to the user with a warning that this will open a portal to the Eye of Cthulhu domain.

Next: Travel to the Eye of Cthulhu Domain

Upon accepting the confirmation the ritual is performed. Visually you see swirling of stars above you which come crashing down on a location near you which forms a portal that can be entered to travel to the Eye of Cthulhu domain.

Completion & Rewards

Upon defeating the Eye of Cthulhu , the Eye of Cthulhu boss domain map can now be dropped as a global item. (1/1000)

You are rewarded with 5000 Experience

You are given a Seeing Eye Amulet

Eldric sets up a new Observatory in Ravencrest and can now be found in Ravencrest
