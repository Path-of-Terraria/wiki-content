Alaric is a wise wizard that serves as the Magic class NPC for Path of Terraria.

He is wearing a blue and yellow wizard robe & hat.

 

Location

Alaric can be found wandering the library in the Town of Ravencrest.

 

First Class Quest - Clumsy Me

Start: Talk to Alaric

Alaric mentions that one of the prized books from his collection was stolen by a Goblin during a recent attack and he is worried the secrets inside may reveal strong magic to the goblins. He asked that you retrieve it from them.

All goblin types will now have a 1/10 chance to drop the “Tome of the Elders”.

Next: Bring Alaric the Tome of the Elders

Alaric thanks you, and offers you a (quote) “mote of his power!"

Rewards

  • The Nova Skill 
  • A Magic rarity Staff
