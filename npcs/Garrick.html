Garrick is a fellow adventurer, beaten up and sorry looking after a recent bout with the King Slime.

Garrick  wears a suit of slightly dented but well-maintained armor, showing signs of previous battles. His broad shoulders are draped with a tattered red cape. Garrick’s face contains messy brown hair and a trimmed beard.

Location

Garrick will appear in Ravencrest's tavern after completing your first class quest.

 

Boss Domain Quest - King Slime

Start: Talk to Garrick

He lets you know that he failed to defeat the King Slime recently but was able to draw map to navigate to the King Slime and take him down. He hands you a King Slime Map. 

Placing the map on a Map Device creates a portal, sending you directly to the King Slime's domain.

Next: Kill the King Slime
Next: Talk to Garrick

Garrick thanks you, promising that you won't outperform him again. The quest ends here.

Reward

  • 500 Experience
  • King Slime map drops randomly from any enemy (1/1000)

 

Dialogue

General Dialogue

“Watch your head. Generally good advice, but especially in the King's domain.”
"Forge your blade as powerful as you can. Cutting through slime is deceptively difficult."
“The ground shakes uncontrollably in the King's domain. As if slime seeps into the rock.”
“Adventurers often fail to tell you of how slimy adventuring is. I am not one of those adventurers. It's extremely slimy.”
 

‘Kill the King’ Quest

Upon starting:
“You may be able to tell - I've not had the best time. Tried to take down the King, almost lost everything. You are the one who restored Ravencrest, yes? Would I ask of you; slay the King Slime on my behalf? I'll reward you greatly. Take this map; I drew it on the long walk back. Good luck!”

After slaying the King Slime:
“I can hardly believe it. Well, you've succeeded where I failed, stranger. I best get back to it; you will not surpass me again. My thanks, however, are in order.”
