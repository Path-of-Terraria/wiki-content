Location

Ravencrest. After completing your first class quest

 

Boss Domain Quest - King Slime

Steps

Upon chatting with Garrick he will let you know that he failed to defeat the king slime recently but was able to obtain a map to navigate to the King Slime.

Garrick will give you a King Slime Domain Map

Use this map in your Map Device to open the portal to the King Slime Domain

 

Reward

500 Experience

The King Slime Domain Map can now be obtained as a Global Drop
