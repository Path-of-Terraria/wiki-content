Thrain Ironfist is a simple dwarf. He works at the Iron Anvil, a blacksmith's forge in Ravencrest.

Thrain wears steel-toed boots, shoulder pads, a leather apron and a work shirt and pants.

Location

He can be found upon entering Ravencrest, slightly left to spawn in the Iron Anvil.

Class Quest - Forging a New Blade

Start: Talk to Thrain Ironfist

Thrain notes the state of the Iron Anvil as it's first found - completely destroyed. He slept through whatever happened. Either way, he would love some materials to help the repair process. 

He asks for you to retrieve the following:

  • 20 Lead or Iron Ore
  • 1 Lead or Iron Hammer
  • 50 Stone
  • 20 Wood

Next: Retrieve the materials and bring them to Thrain

He thanks you for your work, stating that the forge should be more repaired next time you come back. He hands you a blade he had made shortly before the attack as a test product, and asks you to test it on some Zombies. He also asks for a few more materials so the Iron Anvil can be ready to go as soon as possible.

He asks for:

  • 30 Stone Blocks
  • 50 Wood
  • 10 Gold or Platinum Bars

Next: Kill 15 Zombies
Next: Return to Thrain with the materials
