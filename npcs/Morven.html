Morven is an old, grim wanderer who once studied the natural order of the world but was tainted by the corruption that spreads from deep within the land. 

Morven has a tattered cloak with dark green and purple accents, patches of corruption growing on his skin. He carries a twisted staff made from corrupted wood.

 

Location

After the Eye of Cthulhu is defeated Morven can be found in the Underground Corruption . Morven is entombed by a ring of stalagmites that seemingly grew out of nowhere and at incredible speed.

 

Boss Domain Quest - Whispers of the Deep

Starting: Find and Speak to Morven in the Underground Corruption

Morven mentions he was studying the Devourers in the area trying to understand more about the Eater of Worlds and the discovery of Demonite Ore and how useful it could prove to be in the development of more refined materials.
