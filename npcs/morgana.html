Morgana  - The Witch (Summoner)

Morgana is a mysterious figure with raven-black hair cascading down her back and piercing green eyes. She wears a dark, flowing dress with silver accents and a wide-brimmed hat adorned with feathers.

 

Location

Morgana can be found in the town of Ravencrest inside the burrow just outside of town.

 

First Class Quest - The Ancient Art

Start: Talk to Morgana

Morgana mentions she can teach you about the secrets of summoning. She asks for you to retrieve a few items to demonstrate. She hands you a Grimoire, an ancient book of much power, and explains that it needs materials to work. 

She asks you to retrieve the following items:

* 1 Owl Feather

* 1 Pair of Bat Wings

* 1 Scorpion Tail

Next: Bring the Grimoire materials to Morgana

Upon bringing these items she rewards you by providing you the Grimoire Item and she also provides you one full summon set for the Owl summon.

Next: Summon the Owl from the Grimoire
Next: Talk to Morgana

Morgana will comment that, while not flashy, the Owl and the other summons in the Grimoire will help the player a lot. She then ends the quest.

Boss Domain Quest - A Royal Adventure

Start: Talk to Morgana

Morgana mentions she needs a few things from the jungle, namely some stingers. Get her 5 stingers, and she can give you something nice for your time.

Next: Obtain 5 stingers
Next: Return to Morgana with the stingers

She thanks you for the stingers, and gives you a rare Grimoire material for your troubles, unlocking the Hornet grimoire summon.

After thanking you, she asks for one last favor...for now. There's a bee in the jungle, a Queen Bee, that she'd like felled. She says it makes her foraging much less comfortable when a gargantuan bee could bother her at any given moment. She'll reward you kindly for such a deal.

She says to summon your Hornet summon and submerge it in honey for a bit, and a portal to the Queen Bee's lair will automagically open. Good luck! Don't ask how that works.

Next: Kill the Queen Bee
Next: Return to Morgana

Morgana thanks you for your deal, and ends the quest.

Rewards

  • 7500 experience
  • 1 Rare Charm/Amulet
  • Queen Bee map drops randomly from any enemy (1/1000)
