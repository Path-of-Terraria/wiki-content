Morgana  - The Witch (Summoner)

Morgana is a mysterious figure with raven-black hair cascading down her back and piercing green eyes. She wears a dark, flowing dress with silver accents and a wide-brimmed hat adorned with feathers.

 

Location

Morgana can be found in the town of Ravencrest inside the burrow just outside of town.

 

First Class Quest - The Ancient Art

Morgana mentions she can teach you about the secrets of summoning. She asks for you to retrieve a few items to demonstrate.

She asks you to retrieve the following items....

* 1 Owl Feather

* 1 Pair of Bat Wings

* 1 "Any" Scorpion 

Prior to starting the quest the Owl Feather and Bat Wings do not drop

Upon bringing these items she rewards you by providing you the Grimoire Item and she also provides you one full summon set for the Owl summon.

 

The next task is to summon the Owl.  

 

Upon summoning the Owl and talking to Morgana you complete the quest.
