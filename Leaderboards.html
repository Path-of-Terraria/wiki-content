Participating in the Leaderboards

In order to participate in the leaderboards there are a few things you need to do.

  1. Subscribe to the “Path of Terraria Online” Mod
  2. Signup on the website https://pathofterraria.com
  3. Link your Steam Account

 

Posting your Stats

Stats are automatically posted when you save and exit and when your player is saved. Alternatively you can use the /poststats command to submit your stats at any time
